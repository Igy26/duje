Understanding the Basics of Quantum Physics

  • Key Experiments: Explore landmark experiments like the double-slit experiment and their implications for our understanding of reality.
  • Applications in Technology: Learn how quantum mechanics is revolutionizing fields such as computing, cryptography, and medicine.

Learning Outcomes: By the end of this video, viewers will have a foundational understanding of quantum physics and its significance in modern science.
